How to Vote

Enter your name, then choose the movies you want to watch. For every movie you select, choose your enthusiasm level so the group can see how excited you are.

Note: If you check a movie but don't change the stars, we'll automatically give it a default 3-star enthusiasm so your vote still counts.

  1. Enter your name.
  2. Check the movies you'd be interested in watching.
  3. Use the enthusiasm stars to show how excited you are for each selected movie.
  4. Add any food pairing ideas below.
  5. Click "Submit My Votes" to save your choices.

Votes are attached to your name, so you can come back to this page later to view or edit your votes by re-entering your name.
